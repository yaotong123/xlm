Asian investment bank road (on)

The documentary "The Road to Asian Investment Bank" tells the story of the birth of Asia's infrastructure investment bank. Shooting crew to Indonesia, Cambodia, Britain, Japan, Kazakhstan, South Korea, Turkey and India to shoot, to explore the inevitable creation of Asian investment bank; interviewed more than 30 senior Chinese and foreign officials, authoritative scholars and diplomats, economic circles, Investment bank is how to attract the world's 57 countries to join the festivities, during which twists and turns and surprises. From the preparation to the successful opening, the Asian investment bank after 38 months, more than 1,000 days. We record the Asian investment bank set up a major time node for the history of the Asian investment bank to retain the establishment of each key moment in the process.
