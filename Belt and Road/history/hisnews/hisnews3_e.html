"Silk Road" inscription success

Silk Road is a cross-border series of cultural heritage, is a cultural line type. It passes through the route length of about 8700 kilometers, including a total of 33 sites. Among them, there are 22 archaeological sites in China, ancient buildings and other relics, including Henan Province, 4, Shaanxi Province, 7, Gansu Province, 5, Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region 6, the total area of 29825.69 hectares, the total area of the buffer zone 176526.03 hectares. There are eight sites in Kazakhstan, there are three sites in Kyrgyzstan. Silk Road heritage success, is the world's first joint declaration in the form of successful inclusion in the "World Heritage List" Silk Road project, is also China's first cross-border joint declaration of the World Heritage project.
