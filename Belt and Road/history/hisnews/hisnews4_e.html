Maritime Silk Road Documentary

The Silk Road on the Sea is the first large-scale documentary in China with the theme of "Maritime Silk Road" in the 21st century.

As the world's first two thousand years of maritime Silk Road as the theme of the documentary, "Maritime Silk Road" [1] along the country's economic and trade exchanges, cultural exchanges as the starting point to historical exchanges as the background, To live in the story of fresh characters as the main content, open up the history and reality, local and international, with the impact of visual language, delicate and vivid characters story, to create a world-class fine masterpiece, not only show the sea of ​​silk Road once the civilization and glory; more prominent today's style and achievements. There are thousands of years after the legendary story, but also the breath of ordinary people's story.

The filming team spans over 20 countries in Asia, Africa, Europe and Oceania. Among them, ASEAN countries have the closest relationship with the Silk Road in the history and reality. Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Singapore, the Philippines, Myanmar, Vietnam and other countries are the main partner of the film and shooting. Series of documentary "Maritime Silk Road" to vivid and real lens, record these countries people adhere to, explore, struggle, chase the dream of the legend. But also from the shipping, economic and trade, science and technology, culture, immigration, dreams and other multi-faceted, multi-angle show the construction of the 21st century, "Maritime Silk Road" along the national economic development, cultural prosperity produced far-reaching impact.
